Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main motive of Initiation?

The main motive of Initiation is to create a problem repository with proper ground details and also to bring together all the dedicated social workers to tackle the social problems with their new and innovative ideas.

How does Initiation work?

Initiation is an Online platform designed to create a problem repository. The problem submission form you can submit a social problem.The problems will be broadcasted through the main channel of Initiation.To work on a problem you can pitch and discuss the solutions with other members in the Discussion group link given.

How to use the Initiation Channel?

The channel is the main interface of our platform. The submitted problems after checking will be broadcasted through the channel, so that you can view them and choose to work on the issue you like. Also, you will get the Discussion group invitation link with the problem in the Channel.

How to submit a problem?

The targeted problem or issue will be submitted in the Google form designed particularly for the problem submission. You need to provide the proper details of the problem in the form, the description for the same is also written in the form.

How to search for a problem among submitted problems?

All the problems can be accessed from the Google spreadsheet. You can also search through the problems in the Telegram Channel itself using the search option present in the top right corner of the channel.

How will you work on a problem?

The problems will be broadcasted through the main channel. There will be a comment option along with the problem where you can give your suggestions.You can pitch your solutions and discuss with other members. The Initiation Members Group is there for your additional support.
